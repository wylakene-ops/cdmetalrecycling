WHY "FREE DROP-OFF" ITEMS AREN'T PAID

Items like TVs, monitors, and printers contain hazardous materials (lead, mercury, cadmium) that require special handling and disposal — which has a cost. We accept these items responsibly at no charge to you rather than turning them away. It's the right thing to do for the UP and for the environment.

Maximize Your Payout

TIPS TO GET MORE

01

Sort Your Material

Keep CPUs separate from boards, and boards separate from drives. Sorted, clean material always grades higher and pays more than mixed loads.

02

Remove Casings

Strip out the circuit boards from plastic housings before you bring them in. The boards are what we pay for — the plastic isn't worth anything.

03

Bring Large Quantities

Larger quantities often qualify for better rates. If you're clearing out an office or warehouse, call ahead and we can arrange a pickup and special pricing.

04

Call Ahead

Current e-scrap commodity prices fluctuate. Call us first to get the latest rates and confirm we're accepting what you have before making the trip.
